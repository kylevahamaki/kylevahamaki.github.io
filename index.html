Workflow highlights

1. Concept sketching

Define the interaction goals and identify how AR elevates the narrative. I often start with pen-and-paper flows and storyboards.

2. Prototype quickly

Use Three.js, A-Frame, or native WebXR APIs to test the concept on real devices early. Accessibility and performance checks happen from the first build.

3. Iterate with users

Observe how people explore the space. Short feedback loops ensure the experience remains intuitive and inclusive before scaling up.

Resources & downloads
